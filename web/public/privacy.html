Privacy

Last updated: July 2026

What Fatwood stores

When you create an account: your email address and display name (from your sign-in provider). While you use the app: your searches, which results you bookmark or analyze, your saved profile text, your theme preference, and a metered record of AI usage (model, token counts, cost) used to track your free budget. That's it — no ads, no selling data, no tracking pixels.

Where processing happens

Sign-in and credential storage are handled by Microsoft Entra External ID — Fatwood never sees your password. Search queries and your profile text are sent to Anthropic's API to power search interpretation and paper analysis. The site is hosted on Microsoft Azure behind Cloudflare, which processes traffic for security and performance.

Cookies and storage

Fatwood uses browser storage for your sign-in session and theme preference — no third-party advertising or analytics cookies.

Deletion

Want your account and its data gone? Contact the operator and it will be deleted. (A self-service delete button is on the roadmap.)
